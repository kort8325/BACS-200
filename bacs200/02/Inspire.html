As President of the United States for 8 years, president Barrack Obama accomplished so much that no other person in office has. I believe that Obama was an example of how a president should use their position of power for good.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Outrageously courageous - they disturb the status quo and define a new normal
  • Motivate others - people follow a great leader
  • Big footprint - impact on millions and change the world
  • Sacrifice & commitment - their impact usually costs them everything
  • Integrity - they live true to their calling

The qualities listed above are exactly what Obama has shown the world through his leadership. No man in the history of the united states has shown the same qualities such as Obama has, and I believe he is a hero for it. Obama was the first Black President of the United States, and led the country bravely and proudly. Barrack obama is my hero.
